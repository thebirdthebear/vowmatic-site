What Vowmatic does for you

Point it at your footage. Get a ready-to-edit timeline.

🎬

Multicam Autocut

AI edits your ceremony and toasts with angle switching, reaction cuts, and act detection. Vision-powered — it reads the emotion, not just the audio.

🔄

Audio-Based Sync

Cross-correlates audio across all your cameras and board recordings. No manual sync in FCP or Resolve.

🏷️

Clip Organization

AI classifies every clip by event — ceremony, toasts, first look, portraits, getting ready. Browse by keyword in your NLE's sidebar.

⭐

Smart Ratings

Favorites, approves, and flags low-quality clips. Range-based ratings highlight the best moments within long clips.

📖

A-Roll Story Editor

Transcribes audio, scores moments for emotional impact, and lets you build the narrative. Exports a timeline with matching multicam video.

🧠

Learns Your Style

Import your edited timeline back and Vowmatic learns your preferences — camera choices, hold durations, cut timing. Every wedding gets better.
