What Vowmatic does for you

Point it at your footage folder. Get back a ready-to-edit project.

🎬

Multicam Edits

Vowmatic builds a multicam edit of your ceremony, toasts, first look, and dances — switching between cameras at the right moments. You fine-tune from there instead of starting from scratch.

🔄

Automatic Camera Sync

All your cameras and audio recorders get synced together automatically using their audio. No more lining things up by hand in your editor.

🏷️

Every Clip Sorted by Event

AI looks at each clip and labels it — ceremony, toasts, first look, portraits, getting ready, and more. Open your editor and browse clips by category instead of scrolling through hundreds of files.

⭐

Best Moments Highlighted

The best clips get marked as favorites so you can find them fast. Blurry, shaky, or unusable clips get flagged so you can skip them.

📖

Story Builder

Vowmatic listens to your audio, finds the emotional highlights, and helps you pick the best moments for your film. Exports a timeline you can open right in your editor.

🧠

Gets Better Over Time

After you edit, import your finished timeline back into Vowmatic. It learns how you like to cut — your camera preferences, timing, pacing — and improves with every wedding.
