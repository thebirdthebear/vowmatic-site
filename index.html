Multicam Edits

Vowmatic builds a multicam edit of your ceremony, toasts, first look, and dances — switching between cameras at the right moments. You fine-tune from there instead of starting from scratch.

AI Autocut

Picks the best camera angle at every moment — tracking speakers, reactions, and emotion. A rough cut that looks like a real edit.

Every Clip Sorted by Event

AI labels each clip — ceremony, toasts, first look, portraits, getting ready. Browse by category instead of scrolling hundreds of files.

Best Moments Highlighted

The best clips get marked as favorites so you find them fast. Blurry, shaky, or unusable clips get flagged so you can skip them entirely.

Story Builder

Listens to your audio, finds the emotional highlights, and helps you pick the best moments. Exports a timeline right into your editor.

Gets Better Over Time

Import your finished timeline back. It learns your camera preferences, timing, and pacing — and improves with every wedding.
