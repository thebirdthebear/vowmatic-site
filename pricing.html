Questions

Is the free trial really free?

Yes. Your first wedding includes everything — multicam edits, clip sorting, ratings, story builder, the works. No limits, no watermarks, no credit card.

What if I need more weddings than my plan allows?

Extra weddings are $12 each on Pro and Studio plans. Or upgrade to the next tier anytime.

Does my footage get uploaded anywhere?

No. Everything runs on your Mac. Only small thumbnail images get sent to AI for clip sorting. Your video and audio files never leave your computer.

Which editing software works with it?

Final Cut Pro and DaVinci Resolve. The output is built specifically for each editor so everything just works when you import it.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime and keep access through the end of your billing period. Credits never expire.

Do credits expire?

Never. Buy a credit today, use it six months from now.

What cameras does it work with?

Anything that shoots MP4 or MOV. Tested with Sony, Canon, DJI drones, and GoPro. Audio works with MixPre, Zoom, and standard WAV files.

How long does it take?

About 45 minutes total. You'll spend about 5 minutes reviewing clips, then it runs on its own for the rest. Come back to a ready-to-edit project.

Do you have a referral program?

Yes. After your first purchase, you'll get a personal referral code. Share it with a friend — they get an extra free wedding, and you get one too.

Ready to get hours back?

Your first wedding is free. Every feature included. No credit card required.
