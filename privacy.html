Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

The short version:

Your video and audio files never leave your computer. Vowmatic sends only small thumbnail images to AI for clip classification. These thumbnails are processed and discarded — never stored or used for AI training.

1. Who We Are

Vowmatic is operated by Canatella Productions, LLC, based in Cedar Park, Texas. We can be reached at [email protected].

2. What Vowmatic Processes

On Your Computer (never leaves your machine)

  • All video files (.MP4, .MOV, etc.)
  • All audio files (.WAV, .MP3, etc.)
  • Generated FCPXML timeline files
  • Whisper transcriptions (runs locally)
  • Audio cross-correlation sync (runs locally)
  • Rating calculations and cut decisions (runs locally)
  • All footage metadata and project files

Sent to AI Service (via our API proxy)

  • Frame thumbnails: Individual JPEG images extracted from your video footage, typically one frame every 30 seconds. These are small images (under 100KB each, approximately 1280 pixels wide) used for AI-powered clip classification ("is this ceremony or reception?") and quality assessment.
  • Text prompts: Classification instructions and scoring prompts sent alongside thumbnails. These contain no personal information.
  • Transcript text: For story assembly scoring, text transcriptions (generated locally by Whisper) may be sent to the AI service for emotional scoring. These contain spoken words from your wedding audio but no identifying metadata.

What is NOT sent

  • Full video files
  • Full audio files
  • Client names, venue names, or event details
  • File paths or computer information
  • GPS data or location metadata

3. How Thumbnails Are Handled

Thumbnails are transmitted over encrypted HTTPS to our API proxy (hosted on Cloudflare Workers), which forwards them to Anthropic's Claude AI API for analysis. Per Anthropic's API data policy:

  • API inputs are not used to train AI models
  • Data is processed for the request and not retained long-term
  • Anthropic's full privacy policy is available at anthropic.com/privacy

Our API proxy does not store thumbnails. It logs only: timestamp, token identifier, model name, and token counts (for usage tracking and billing). No image data is logged.

4. Your API Token

Your API token identifies your Vowmatic account for usage tracking and access control. It is stored locally in ~/.wedding_analyzer/config.json on your computer. We use it to:

  • Authenticate your API requests
  • Track usage for billing (subscription or per-wedding credits)
  • Enforce rate limits

5. Website Data Collection

Our website (vowmatic.ai) collects:

  • Email address — when you download Vowmatic or subscribe, to send your license key and updates
  • Google Analytics — anonymous page views and interactions to understand how people use our website
  • Meta Pixel — anonymous conversion tracking for our advertising
  • Stripe — payment information for subscriptions, processed entirely by Stripe (we never see your card number)

6. Your Rights

You can request deletion of any data we hold about you by emailing [email protected]. Since Vowmatic processes footage locally, uninstalling the app removes all local processing data.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated via email. The "last updated" date at the top always reflects the current version.
