Certain AI features require an internet connection to communicate with our API proxy, which routes requests to third-party AI services.

6. Data Processing

Vowmatic processes your video footage locally on your computer. Your video and audio files are never uploaded to our servers. To enable AI-powered features (clip classification, vision scoring), small frame thumbnails (individual JPEG images, typically under 100KB each) are transmitted to our API proxy, which forwards them to Anthropic's AI service for analysis. These thumbnails are:

  • Transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Processed and discarded by the AI service — not stored permanently
  • Not used to train AI models (per Anthropic's API data policy)
  • Sampled at approximately one frame every 30 seconds of footage

For full details on data handling, see our Privacy Policy.
